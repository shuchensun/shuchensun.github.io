Research Profile

Long-horizon embodied intelligence for general-purpose robots

My research focuses on memory-augmented Vision-Language-Action systems that connect short-term perception with long-term task reasoning. I am especially interested in temporally aware decision-making, multi-task robot learning, policy generalization, and robust deployment in complex real-world environments.
